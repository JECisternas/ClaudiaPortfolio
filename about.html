Claudia loves expressing herself through creating pieces of art. Whether is by making a great design piece or an outstanding photograph, she finds her artistic outlet to be the most compatible with her personality.

Bright and colorful, or a balanced black and white: these characteristics also form part of who Claudia is. A happy and energetic person, but also someone connected to their emotions.

From an early age she demonstrated interest in the visual arts. As a kid, her favorite class was always Art, in which she always showed talent and hard work.

Graduated from the University of Utah with a Bachelor of Fine Arts, she was able to expand her artistic knowledge and develop her artistic skills.
